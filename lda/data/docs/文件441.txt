The validity period of visas to the United States subject to administrative review has been extended. According to a reporter from Xinhua News Agency, a reporter from the US embassy in China learned that now, exchange visitors, internal transfer personnel, business travelers, and sightseeing tourists who need additional administrative review for visa applications to the United States A multiple-entry visa valid for one year can be obtained. According to Shi Jianbang, deputy consul of the US embassy in China, this type of visa was previously valid for three months; after the visa validity period is extended to one year, the above-mentioned applicants can travel to the United States without restrictions within one year.

Digital China takes over the core collection and management system of land tax in Shenzhen. Yangcheng Evening News reporter Han Ping reported: Digital China Holdings Co., Ltd., China's largest IT service provider, officially announced on the 17th that it has officially taken over the sub-provincial level of China's third largest land tax revenue of over 100 billion. City - Shenzhen's core land tax collection and management system, the company will further expand the market in Shenzhen and the entire South China region. This is another "core collection and management system" of local tax above the sub-provincial level received by Digital China after winning the bid for the Hainan Provincial Land Tax Project. Guo Wei, Chairman and CEO of Digital China, said that Digital China will increase its market expansion efforts in Shenzhen.

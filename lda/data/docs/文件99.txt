Iverson's arrival in Turkey is delayed by 2 days, fans need to wait longer if they want to meet their idols Istanbul is 2 days later than originally planned. According to the original plan, Iverson will appear at the club on Saturday, local time, and participate in the fan meeting at the team's home court. Besiktas said on its official website on Friday that Iverson's arrival would be delayed because the answer was still not ready in some places. Now the team has also given his new arrival time, which is November 8, local time. Iverson is currently on a two-year contract with Besiktas for a total of $4 million. After the NBA could not find a team willing to accept him, he also took a different path and went to Europe to continue his career. (Wolfman)

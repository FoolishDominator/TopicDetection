News: Chinese Students Awarded as British International Student Star A few days ago, Chinese student Li Tieqiang from Zhanjiang, Guangdong won the title of International Student Star of the Year 2009 in Northeast England in the 7th International Student Star Awards 2009 organized by the British Council . This Grand Prix is the largest competition in the UK to enhance the influence and contribution of international students studying in the UK. A total of more than 1,500 international students from 118 countries participated, and 12 international students from different parts of the UK, including Li Tieqiang, won the regional award. .
